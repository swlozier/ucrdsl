Welcome!

This website is a collection of notes and resources for the new Scholars Lab being created at UC Riverside.

As a project of the UCR Library, the Scholars Lab will be located in the Rivera Library, and this website serves as a public workspace for its development.

The information collected here is neither final nor definitive and is constantly in flux. This digital workspace will eventually be archived within the official website for the Scholars Lab.
